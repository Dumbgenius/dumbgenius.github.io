About
This solver can solve most (read: probably some) GCSE quantitative analysis titration questions without too much issue.
Warning: Does not work with formulae with brackets!
For formulae with brackets, eg. Ca(OH)2, type them as CaOHOH or CaO2H2.
Adding bracket parsing is on the list of things to do.
